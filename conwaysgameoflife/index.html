WHO WAS JOHN CONWAY?

John Horton Conway was born December 26 1937 in Liverpool. He was an English mathematician active in the theory of finite groups, knot theory, number theory, combinatorial game and coding theory.

In 1970, Conway created a cellular automation called The Game of Life. It is what is referred to a zero-player game, meaning that its evolution is determined by its initial state

RULES:

If the cell is alive and has 2 or 3 neighbors, then it remains alive. Otherwide, it dies.

If the cell is dead and has exactly 3 neighbors, then it comes to life. Otherwide, it remains dead.
